About Us


Welcome to Cars in USA, located in Denver, Colorado! At Cars in USA, we proudly showcase the finest vehicles from the Big Three Detroit automakers—Ford Motor Company, General Motors, and Chrysler Corporation. In recent years, many car enthusiasts have turned their attention to vehicles from the 1970s, 1980s, 1990s, and beyond. Automobile reviewer Lakindu Kaveesha recognized the lack of a dedicated platform focused solely on reviewing cars. To fill this gap, he created Cars in USA. The cars featured on our website are exciting, fun, interesting, or quirky, all representing the modern era of automobiles.


Cars in USA ignites the passion for Detroit iron, celebrating the enduring legacy of Ford, GM, and Chrysler – the automakers that forged the American automotive landscape. We're a brotherhood of enthusiasts who live and breathe the raw power, iconic designs, and rich history of these American-made machines. From the rumble of a V8 to the gleam of chrome, we appreciate the unique character and performance that sets Detroit steel apart. Driving a Ford, GM, or Chrysler isn't just about getting from point A to point B; it's a connection to our heritage, a way to support American ingenuity, and a testament to the enduring spirit of American manufacturing. It's about the pride you feel behind the wheel, knowing you're part of a legacy that put America on wheels and continues to drive innovation. For many of us, it's a personal connection to family, memories, and the open road – a symbol of milestones achieved and the unwavering spirit of American automotive engineering. We believe these vehicles embody the resilience, quality, and relentless pursuit of innovation that defines American manufacturing, and at Cars in USA, we're here to celebrate every roaring engine and every mile of American road.
